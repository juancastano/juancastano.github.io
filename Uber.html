Uber Explore: An App Redsign

Personal Project

Goals & Context

What is the goal?

Our goal is to create a turn key solution to exploring cities.

What problem does this project solve?

As of now, ridesharing users struggle to compare travel estimates of costs between competing venues and often have to use multiple applications to find and assess potential destinations.

What is the vision?

Whether it is exploring a new city or discovering a new spot in town, by integrating crowd-sourced reviews of locations along with travel and price estimates, Uber can create a seamless and efficient travel experience.

Annotated Screenshots

Home Page

By overlaying the Uber map with interactive pins and an Explore Card, Uber Explore does more than provide users rides, it gives users the chance to choose their own adventure. With just a tap on a pin or a simple swipe upwards, Uber Explore unlocks a world of experiences.

Category Selection

Whatever the mood or goal, users can choose between Uber Explore’s many categories to find their perfect experience.

Location Results

Specifically tailored to satisfy user needs, Uber Explore allows users to sort location cards by price, proximity, and rating. By providing key decision-making facts on the cards, users can easily and quickly make actionable decisions.

Location Information

Uber Explore does more than give a description of potential destinations. At a glance, users can contribute and pull from categorized ratings to find their ideal destination.
