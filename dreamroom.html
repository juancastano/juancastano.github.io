Dream Room

CS22: 3D Digital Modeling

Our culminating project for Dartmouth Computer Science 22: 3D Digital Modeling, was to design our “dream room” in Maya. With graduation soon approaching, I played around with what my dream apartment would look like as a start my career. I’m personally a fan of minimalistic design, so I styled the entire room to build a clean aesthetic. We used a combination of Maya, Mental Ray and Photoshop to model, texture, and render the complete the final product.
