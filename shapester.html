Shapester

iOS game on App Store

I teamed up with a developer friend over my freshman summer with the goal of making a simple, addicting app. Shapester was the end product of our brainstorming. As shapes fall from the sky, users must swipe up to match the shape, in addion to swiping left and right to align the falling shape with the "shapester". Users continue to rack up points as they match shapes, and the game ends once a falling shape make it past the "shapester".

Check out the Shapester on the app store! https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/shapester/id1141694509?mt=8
